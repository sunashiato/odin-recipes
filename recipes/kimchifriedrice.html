Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup kimchi ((*see note), cut into thumbnail size pieces)
  • 150 g bacon ((5.3 ounces), cut into thumbnail size pieces)
  • 3 cups steamed short grain rice
  • x 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup Kimchi juice ((*see note) â€“ this liquid is from the bottom of the kimchi container)
  • 2-3 Tbsp gochujang
  • 1/2 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 Tbsp toasted sesame seeds (to garnish)
  • 1/2 stalk green onion (to garnish, thinly sliced (optional))
  • roasted seasoned seaweed (optional)
  • cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup water (optional)

Steps

  1. On medium high heat preheat a pan/wok and once heated, add the cooking oil and spread it well with a spatula.
  2. Add 1/2 tsp minced garlic, stir it fast for about 10 seconds. Then add 150g bacon and stir it well until half of it is cooked.
  3. Meanwhile, start cooking two fried eggs
  4. Add 1 cup kimchi and stir until 80% of it is cooked.
  5. Add the rice, 1/4 cup kimchi juice, and 2-3 tbsp gochujang. Mix all of them together well and thoroughly. Add 1/4 cup water if necessary.
  6. Add 1/2 tbsp sesame oil and mix them well. Remove from the heat. Sprinkle some cheese (optional).
  7. Serve the Kimchi fried rice on a plate. Garnish with the sesame seeds, green onion and seaweed strips. (Garnish is all optional). Place the cooked egg on top. Enjoy
